I began this project after I purchased a cricut machine to make things for my family and I. My friends began expressing interest in custom items for them and their families. I have since experimented with using my cricut cutter to make many different items such as shirts, etched glasses, wooden and canvas signs, can and bottle sleeves, and vinyl decals, to name a few. Since I work alone, I can handle most projects, unless they are high volume projects with tough time constraints. Contact me with your ideas and we can get started!

Welcome to my site! Feel free to explore around, and do not hesitate to contact me with any questions you may have! I can be reached via my phone number or email address, both of which can be found on the contact page!
